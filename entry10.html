Influences

Sex And The City

The show *Sex and the City* stars four girlfriends navigating their love life in New York. When moving, I found it to be a comforting show to romanticize my new life. They explore different boroughs, events, and parks. They give ideas for events and activities to do with friends. Overall, the show has shaped my fashion, interior design choices, and upbeat mindset.

How To Loose a Guy in 10 Days

The movie How to Loose a Guy in 10 Days also takes place in New York. The main character works as a writer testing an experiment on traits women have that would loose a guy. The playful humor and impulsivity captures the wild fun found in the city.
